Fruit and Yogurt smoothie

This yogurt smoothie recipe is delicious! You may substitute the strawberries for any other berries or fruit.

Ingredient required

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Combine strawberries, banana, yogurt in blender
  2. Add pineapple juice, orange juice, milk in blender
  3. Add sugar
  4. Blend it until smooth
  5. Serve it
